# Privacy Policy — NyanTower: Cat Defense Squad

Last updated: 2026-05-02

This policy explains how the mobile game **NyanTower: Cat Defense Squad** ("the App") handles user data.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App does not request or collect personally identifiable information you would manually enter (name, email, phone number, etc.). For service operation it automatically processes:

- **Firebase anonymous user ID (UID)**: a randomly generated identifier with no link to your real identity or contact details.
- **Game progress data**: in-game currencies, upgrade/equipment/unit ownership, current wave, farm/mission/pass progress, etc.

The App does **not** collect: location, contacts, photos/media, call logs, SMS, advertising IDs (IDFA/AAID).

## 2. Purpose of Use

- Provide cloud save and cross-device sync
- Monitor service stability and respond to incidents
- Detect and prevent abuse

## 3. Storage and Processing

- Data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication).
- Sub-processor: Google LLC, under Firebase Data Processing Terms.
- All transport is encrypted via HTTPS.

## 4. Retention and Deletion

- Cloud save persists after uninstall (so reinstalls can restore progress).
- To delete your data, email us with your anonymous UID. We will delete within 30 days.

## 5. Third-Party Sharing

The App does not sell or share user data with advertising networks or other third parties. (No advertising SDK is integrated in the current build.)

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
